Marble, Granite & Quartz in London ‚Äî Supply, Fabrication, and Installation

We fabricate and install premium stone for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial interiors across London. Whether refitting a family kitchen in Wimbledon, a hotel reception in Mayfair, or a development in Canary Wharf, we manage survey, templating, cutting, logistics, and install to programme.

What We Deliver

High-grade slabs from vetted partners, CAD/CAM tolerances to the millimetre, and installers trained for tight-access period homes and time-critical retail sites. We hand over sealed, cleaned surfaces with care guidance.

Materials We Recommend

Quartz for busy kitchens; Granite for heat and scratch resistance (great outdoors); Marble for bathrooms and tables; and Quartzite for marble aesthetics with higher abrasion resistance.

End-to-End Process

  1. Consultation & Specification: define brief, budget, and timeline.
  2. Templating: digital laser capture ensures precision.
  3. CNC Fabrication: mitres, grooves, radius corners, cut-outs, sculpted edges.
  4. Installation & Sign-off: protect, fit, seam, seal, polish, and brief care.

Care & Maintenance

Quartz: mild soap and water; avoid harsh abrasives and direct high heat. Granite & Marble: seal on install and reseal periodically. See our care guide.
